Professor Dr. Munshi Md. Mojibur Rahman

MBBS, MCPS, FCPS
Speciality -Cardiovascular Surgery
Department -Cardiac Surgery



Professor Dr. Munshi Md. Mojibur Rahman graduated from Dhaka Medical College in 1981. Then he joined Army Medical core of Bangladesh Armed Forces in February 1983.

He passed Grading Exam in Surgery in AMC. After post-graduation in BCPS he was trained in Cardiovascular Surgery from Germany (University Hosp, Hamburg), Pakistan (Armed Forces Medical Institute, Islamabad), Saudi Arabia, Singapore and India. He Passed MCPS & FCPS. He worked as Examiner of Surgery & CV Surgery of BCPS & BSMMU. He was teacher of AFMC, CMH, BCPS & BSMMU.

He became Major General. He was chief Surgeon & chief Cardiovascular Surgeon of CMH, then Commandant of AFMC, then Consultant Surg. Gen & finally DGMS of Bangladesh Armed Forces. He wrote about 30 articles in National and International journals.
